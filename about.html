About the SAIYANS ⚔️

We are a group of people dedicated to ANIME 🔥 but we like to consider ourselves as an Association. We spend most of our time talking about ANIME, a lot ,when I say a lot, i mean A LOT. 💥

Our Vision 🌟

To introduce change to the whole world through ANIME. This world knows too little about ANIME, so we have taken it upon ourselves to introduce them to the wonders of ANIME! 🌍

Our Motto 🗣️

WAKE UP TO REALITY. Just like Madara Uchiha told the world to Wake Up To Reality, we want to do the same with people, this motto is used since we are telling the world to wake up to the wonderful endeavors of ANIME! 👀

📜 Our History 📜

2020 - The Beginning

THE SAIYANS was founded by a group of passionate anime enthusiasts who wanted to create a space where fellow fans could connect and share their love for anime.

2021 - First Major Event

Hosted our first virtual anime watch party with over 500 participants. This marked the beginning of our tradition of community events.

2022 - Global Expansion

Expanded our community internationally, welcoming members from over 50 countries and establishing regional chapters.

2023 - Official Recognition

Received official recognition as a premier anime community organization and partnered with major streaming platforms.

2024 - Digital Transformation

Launched our comprehensive online platform with forums, resources, and interactive features for members worldwide.

2025 - Community Milestone

Reached 50,000 active members and established our first physical community center for local meetups and events.
