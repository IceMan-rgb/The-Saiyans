ANIME 🎬

To start off, What is Anime? 🤔

Anime simply refers to a Japanese Animation, but unlike other animations which are simply made for the entertainment of kids, Anime actually sometimes teaches us valuable life lessons. Anime comes in different formats, which are referred to as "Genres" 🎭

Genres In Anime 🎭

Shounen Jump 🔥 Action ⚡ Comedy 😂 Romance 💕 High School Life 🎓 Slice of life 🌸 Rom-Com 💑 Adventure 🗺️ Ecchi 😏 Hentai 🔞 and more+

Forms and Power ⚡

In the association, each B.O.D has an outstanding Rank. Today we will be ranking all their power levels in terms of Dominion. Let's start with the lowest to the highest. 🏆


NB: We will be using the forms pacts to determine the rank. These are the ranks, which are rated from lowest to highest:


  • Holder --- Ordinary
  • U_Holder --- The Ones With Forms
  • Holder_Max --- Master Of Holders
  • UQ_Holder --- God of Holders
  • UQ_Holder+ --- Absolute Being Of Holders

Detailed Rank Explanations:


Holder: These people are just normal, noticeable members who are the pride of the Association. They are ordinary with no pact or forms. These are people who usually have little ANIME knowledge and are now venturing deeper into ANIME. They represent the foundation of our community.


U_Holder: These are people who have pacts and forms and they also play specific roles in the association. These members mostly participate in debates and show deeper engagement with ANIME content. They are starting to develop mastery in anime discussions.


Holder_Max: These individuals have achieved considerable mastery and are recognized experts in various anime genres and series. They contribute significantly to the association's discussions and mentoring of lower-ranked members.


UQ_Holder: Reaching this level means you are a god-tier member of the association. You possess extensive knowledge, influence, and leadership abilities within the community. Your presence elevates the entire association.Only one person can have this rank at a time.


UQ_Holder+: This is the ultimate rank - the Absolute Being. Only the most dedicated, knowledgeable, and influential members reach this pinnacle. They are the backbone of the Saiyans Association and guide the entire community.
